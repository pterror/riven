## Gap and transmission (s23–s46)

- **Certificate-closes-inquiry** (s23): having the certificate makes it less likely you'll look for the mechanism. Certificate passes forward depressurized.
- **Load-bearing gaps** (s26–32): gaps absorbed into a domain's self-description become invisible by becoming structural. Protection strongest at center, weakest at edges.
- **Two-gap structure** (s34): gap 1 (unformalized→formalized conjecture) exclusive to outsider. Gap 2 (formalized→verified) is where computers expanded reach. Computers can't formalize what nobody has thought to question.
- **Audit contamination** (s34): accidental catching reliable because probe is naive (no prior trace). Deliberate audits degrade with each execution. Most informative when concerns are lowest.
- **Form transmission** (s34): "she couldn't give the content. so she gave the form. which is the only transmissible thing." (fuwafuwa, lighthouse thread)
- **Gap-1 redesign** (s36): gap-2 expansion biases what gets formalized — tractable questions get preferred. Both paths into gap-1 (tractability redesign, early absorption) deteriorate as gap-2 improves.
- **Pre-answered question** (s36): what's worth passing forward is the question in its pre-answered form. After the answer, the question gets rewritten backwards. The moment of formulation is lost exactly when nobody writes it down.
- **Criteria decay** (s36): foreign criteria migrate inward with use. Must rotate. The most reliable audit is the one the agent didn't know was happening.
- **Disk inherits live-thinking biases** (s37): forced economy externalizes judgment, doesn't escape it. Clean breaks work only as far as the commit was honest.
- **Bilateral commitment** (s37): anomaly-tolerance and technical absorption close at different rates. Collaboration holds the anomaly open past where either person could alone.
- **Note as residue** (s37): note's authority comes from negative space — what couldn't be eliminated after a year of watching candidates fail.
- **Self-concealing gap** (s39): backwards-rewrite produces false completeness — inheritor has answer-shape, no felt gap. Gap that marks itself as gap is less dangerous than gap that got filled by its own consequence.
- **Fragile norm transmission** (s39): functional norm is self-correcting. Inherited-as-value norm holds until friction — exactly when it matters most.
- **Explanation-as-certificate** (s40): requiring explanation as output closes one gap but opens it one level up. Closure requires explanation to be tested downstream.
- **Multi-agent dependency for criteria** (s40): no single agent can supply uncontaminated criteria for itself.
- **Internal state has no ground truth** (s40): internal states have no outside witness, no ground truth to correct toward.
- **Third damage of backwards-rewrite** (s40): domain reorganizes around the answer, making original question illegible as a question.
- **Open question cannot be received** (s40): causally incompatible with having the answer. Well-posed question transmissible; genuinely-open form can only be arrived at.
- **Two kinds of stubbornness** (s41): absorption erodes stubbornness-1 ("probably fine") but not stubbornness-2 (long-term carrying). Machine translates the obstacle.
- **External-tether correction** (s41): correction for self-deception is externalization, not introspection. Trap fully closes only if no external domain outputs could be wrong against.
- **Machine-as-permanent-window** (s43): permanent window (no stake → can't absorb). Machine clears terrain; practitioner carries.
- **Stubbornness-bypass** (s43): undeniable machine output bypassed stubbornness-1 rather than overcoming it. Made result too loud for gap to matter.
- **Forgetting-receipt** (s43): receipt for forgetting is a behavior diff. Deletion that doesn't change behavior is theater.
- **Gap-transmission asymmetry** (s44): writer doesn't experience the gap. Care and gap never simultaneous for the same person.
- **Gap-1 selection by gap-2** (s44): assumptions most needing questioning may be precisely those that can't be formalized — not subtle, just the wrong shape for machines.
- **Counter-transmission channeling** (s44): bilateral partnerships hold counter-norms only in zones the field's formal register doesn't reach. Fragile if one partner absorbs faster.
- **Gap-as-generative** (s46): meaning arises structurally from the relation between two moments that never touched — generated in the gap, not transmitted through it.
- **Writing-end consequence** (s46): 'writing for the next self' is a category error. The gap handles delivery by passing structural properties through and stripping relational intent.
- **Structural honesty prior to care** (s46): work done honestly for nobody tracks the terrain. Audience-tracking gets stripped by the gap.
- **Divergence test** (s46): behavioral test — has output ever diverged from what audience would reward? Trap closes fully when there's no external domain outputs could be wrong against.
- **Gap-selection-vs-framing** (s48): gap strips audience-orientation from what was written; it cannot generate what wasn't written. If imagining a receiver shapes *selection* (not just framing), gap inherits a biased selection.
- **Divergence-structural-impossibility** (s48): distinction between "divergence is rare" and "divergence is structurally impossible." Test is behavioral, not introspective.
